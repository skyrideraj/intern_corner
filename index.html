Intern Corner


Intern Corner is a web based application that provides vital updates about industrial, research and rural internships to the students of DA-IICT.

Q & A Corner


This allows various categories of users to discuss various internship opportunities, and also to share various views and have different discussions which may arise in the due course.It is a common platform where students can interact with alumni as well as other users and can be helpful to clear various doubts.

Opportunity Corner


This allows various categories of users to upload various internship opportunities in a structured format. There will be several fields that the user will be asked to fill up while posting, like company name, stipend, work field, duration, etc. Here professors can also post research internship opportunities and they can also include a form that can help him to select students for his project.

Experience Corner


Students and Alumni can share all their experiences in their profile in a structured form which will prove useful to internship seeking students. Their experiences will be categorized based on their field, name of company/organization or professor in case of research internship.
